a lil bit about us

A Neighborhood coffee shop for everything from your not-so-average cup of joe to your lattes, cappuccinos and cold-press toddies. We serve all local products and a healthy dose of good attitude - we leave the snobbery to the coffee, not the customers.

why we started

After being laid off from her job, proprietor Amanda Horne decided to pursue her dream of opening her own coffee shop. In February 2009, she opened GrindHouse Coffee to cater to the North Portland neighborhood surrounding Rosa Parks Way and Denver Avenue.Amanda has been a barista since 1998 and over the years developing her passion for great coffee.
